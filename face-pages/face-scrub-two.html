Morning Coffee Facial Scrub

Recipe by Leesa

Super cheap, easy, simple and most importantly natural. Check out my YouTube channel Leesa Unique for all kinds of fun and lifestyle tips.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp Coffee grounds- Rich in antioxidants, the coffee will boost blood flow and increase the production of collogen.
  • 1 tbsp Sugar- Removes dead skin cells also naturally hydrates the skin.
  • 1 tbsp Bicarb soda- Lifts oil out of the skin and removes dead skin cells.
  • 1 tbsp Honey- I've just used regular store bought honey for this scrub, it has antibacterial properties and also has antioxidants for a double boost.

Instructions

  1. Mix all the ingrediants together and add 1 tsp of water.

Scoop out with a spoon and rub in circular motions on your face, avoid the eyes. Rinse with warm water.
Use each morning as part of your wake up routine and if you have time leave it for 10 mins to dry before you wash it off.
This amount will last a couple of days, if you want to make more to last a week keep it in the fridge in an air tight container.
