Pineapple Glow Face Mask

Recipe by Himani

Hi, I'm Himani! My mission is to help you look and feel beautiful, radiant and confident by being in alignment with nature. I empower women to go natural with their beauty and lifestyle. My DIY home remedies are based on an ancient holistic system called Ayurveda. It is over 5000 years old. Most of the DIYs on my YouTube channel Himani Wright have been passed down to me through generations.

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp Pineapple juice- Pineapple juice is the storehouse of vitamin C, bromelain and antioxidants, which are extremely beneficial in the treatment and prevention of acne, fine lines, sun damaged and uneven skin toning, etc. Bromelain softens the skin and used against skin inflammation and swelling.
  • 2-3 Lemon juice- Skin Brightener. Lemons are rich in vitamin C and citric acid, so they can help brighten and lighten your skin when used over time.
  • 1 tsp Facial clay- any kind- French Green Clay, Kaolin Clay, Multani Mitti, etc. It is detoxifying and absorbent, find them online and in health stores.

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients together in a bowl.

Apply to the face and leave for 10 minutes. Remove with a cotton pad dipped in warm water then rinse the rest with water.
If you don't have the clay this can work as a great toner, just make sure you moisterise after using it.
Use a few times a week.
