Rolled Oats Clearing Face Mask

Recipe by Anita

As most of you know from my YouTube channel AnitaSamantha, I am all about natural remedies to beauty, health, and healing and I love to share all of the secrets and tips that have helped me throughout life. I also have a passion for the environment we place ourselves in whether it be through traveling, home decor, or unique aesthetics that inspire me.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Rolled Oats- Oatmeal has rich anti-oxidant properties and helps undo damage done to your skin by pollution, UV rays and chemicals. Oats helps reduce itching and inflammation and can be used to calm eczema and heal psoriasis.
  • 1 tsp Baking soda- Baking soda is an excellent remedy for pimples, blackheads, and acne. It is a mild exfoliant that helps remove dead skin and cleans your pores. It does this while helping dry out and heal existing acne. Baking soda also has antibacterial properties which prevent breakouts.

Instructions

  1. Mix the oats with 5 tsp's of warm water, preferably boiled water that has cooled. Leave for 3 minutes until the oats soften.
  2. Mix in the baking soda and leave fot 5 more minutes.

Apply to your face with fingers or a make-up brush, apply 3 layers and leave on for 10 mins or until dry. Rinse of with warm water and a face cloth. Splash your face with cold water.
You skin and pimples may look red for a bit but will soon calm and should educe in size throughout the day.
Use this mask several times a week even when skin looks clear to help prevent breakouts.
