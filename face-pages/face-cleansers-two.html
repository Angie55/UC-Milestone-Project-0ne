Milk and Tumeric Facial Cleanser

Recipe by Himani

Hi, I'm Himani! My mission is to help you look and feel beautiful, radiant and confident by being in alignment with nature. I empower women to go natural with their beauty and lifestyle. My DIY home remedies are based on an ancient holistic system called Ayurveda. It is over 5000 years old. Most of the DIYs on my YouTube channel Himani Wright have been passed down to me through generations.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Milk Powder- Contains lactic acid that helps to mildly exfoliate, deep cleanse, and brighten the skin. It fights acne as well as wrinkles.
  • 1/2 cup Chickpea Flour- Rolled oats will also work great, both deep clean the skin as well as exfoliate it really well.
  • 2 tsp Turmeric-(Optional ingredient) Anti-bacterial, anti-oxidant and anti-inflammatory, so it treats acne, wrinkles, and any skin conditions.

Instructions

  1. Mix the ingredients together in a bowl

Store it in an airtight container, the shelf life is about one year.
Take out 1-2 teaspoons of this powder, and mix with water to make a paste. Apply all over your face. Massage gently using circular motions. Please don't apply it around the eye area. Wash it off warm water, and splash your face with cold water.
You can apply it every day (once a day).
