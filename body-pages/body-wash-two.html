Nourishing Foaming Body Wash

Recipe by Chessi

A creative jack of all trades. My YouTube channel Chessi serves up a little bit of everything. I like to keep what goes onto my skin and into my body as simple as possible. It's so easy to make great skincare using all natural ingredients that could easily compete with the top brands.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup Coconut oil- Benefits include reducing inflammation, keeping skin moisturized and helping heal wounds. The medium-chain fatty acids found in coconut oil also possess antimicrobial properties that can help treat acne.
  • 1/3 cup Vitamin E oil- Protects the skin against cell mutation in the sun and pollution. It's also an anti-inflammatory, so it calms and hydrates sensitive skin.
  • 1/4 cup Raw honey- Is naturally antibacterial, so it's great for acne treatment and prevention. Full of antioxidants, it is great for slowing down aging. It is extremely moisturizing and soothing.
  • 1/2 cup Castile soap- Castile soap is a concentrated vegetable-based soap made of ingredients you can actually pronounce. This soap is gentle on the skin and effective in the fight against dirt and unwanted germs.
  • 5 drops Eucalyptus oil- The chemical components in eucalyptus essential oil contribute to its position as a purifying, cleansing, clarifying, and immune-boosting oil ideal for use on the skin.
  • 1 tbsp Rosemary- Nutrients of rosemary can help protect skin cells from damage often caused by the sun and free radicals. Since rosemary has natural antiseptic properties, it's a superior disinfectant for our skin and hair.

Instructions

  1. Chuck all the ingredients together in a bowl and mix them together.

Pour the recipe into a pump dispenser for easy use in the bath or shower.
Give it a good shake before each use.
